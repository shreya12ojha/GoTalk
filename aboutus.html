Team Hacktivists

Hey, there! We're the "Hacktivists", the team who developed GoTalk. All of us are proud members of the Cohort 6 of the Google WE program, conducted by NSE Talentsprint and sponsored by Google. We're a group of four college students from all across India who met at the Google WE June Bootcamp 2024. We decided to develop GoTalk as our mandatory project after the bootcamp. Developing GoTalk has been an incredible journey, a blend of victories and defeats with lots to learn along the way, and we really hope you're happy to see the result!
